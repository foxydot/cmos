30 SECOND THEME INSTALLATION: 
1. Log into your WordPress Admin, select "Appearance" and then "Add New Themes".
2. After the page loads, select "Upload" and browse for the "side-blog.zip" theme file.
3. Click "Install Now" and wait for the page to reload.
4. Under the "Appearance" menu, click "Themes" and select the theme you wish to activate.
6. Configure your Theme Options and Widgets.

SIDE BLOG SETUP:
1. Click "Appearance" then "Widgets".
2. Drag the "P75 - Side Blog" widget to any sidebar you wish to display the side blog on.
3. Configure the side blog widget options.
